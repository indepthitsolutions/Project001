About

When I was in 6th Standard that time I wanted to become a director. From the age of 12 my mind set was clear to achieve something in Film/Television industry. I started achieving my goal step by step. Early in 2019, I gave interviews in many companies but they didn’t allow me because of my inexperience but it is said failure is the first step of Success. In mid-2019, I gave interview in Balaji telefilms for Asst. Director with no experience but all I had was my passion towards my dream & hardworking.


In only one & half month I got promoted from TCR Asst. Director to First Assistant Director. After that I’ve done four shows with balaji telefilms named as Kawach Mahashivratri Season 2, Be Panha Pyaar, Yeh hai Chahatein & Prem Bandhan. Just give me a chance to direct something like Short films, Sketch videos, Web series & yes I can prove myself. My work techniques make me different from others & I will never let anyone down who trust me on my work. With good track record there are few people who don’t like my work but there are a lot of people who love & praise my work from spot boy to DIRECTOR.
